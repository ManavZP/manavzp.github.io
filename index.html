Jellyfish

Although I have absolutely no proof (yet), I am 100% convinced that jellyfish are NOT of Earthly origins. I mean, they are sentient plastic bags of nothing that glow in the dark, float through the water, can eat fish, and yet they don't have a brain. How?! Not to mention, they look incredibly creepy, with their long, thin tendrils and bulbous, blob heads. No, they are definitely aliens, and I intend to prove it.
