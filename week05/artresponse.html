The Dollie Clone Series

The Dollie Clone series is a project created by Lynn Hershman. 2 dolls, named CybeRoberta and Tillie the Telerobotic Doll (evil twin) had cameras inseted into their eyes, and they were put on display.

People could go to the website and see "live video feed" of what the dolls were seeing.

To quote net.art, the explored themes centered around "Surveillance, the fluidity of identity, clones and cyborgs, and the performance of gender and the the self"
