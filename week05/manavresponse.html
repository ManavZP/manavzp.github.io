The Dollie Clone Series

First reaction: what the heck

A highlight of this project was the idea that looking through the eyes of the dolls
using a camera is akin to being a cyborg. This is an incredibly interesting viewpoint
to look at through the lense of modern day technology, where we actually have virtual reality
machines that allow us to essentially become "cyborgs." Even on our phones, most people have
the capability to use augmented reality and can use that tech in creative ways to mimic the
project in more advanced ways.
