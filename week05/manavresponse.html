The Dollie Clone Series

A highlight of this project was the idea that looking through the eyes of the dolls using a camera is akin to being a cyborg. This is an incredibly interesting viewpoint to look at through the lense of modern day technology, where we actually have virtual reality machines that allow us to essentially become "cyborgs." Even on our phones, most people have the capability to use augmented reality and can use that tech in creative ways to mimic the project in more advanced ways. For example, making a robot with vr-capable cameras in it's eyes and then connecting that to a vr headset, allowing you to control and see through the robot, fully immersed.
